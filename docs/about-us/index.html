who we are

scinteco is a Vienna-based software company that was founded in 2011 by a team of experienced software developers and architects with a focus on designing and developing IT solutions, applications, and products for the pharmaceutical industry. The team has worked for the pharmaceutical industry since 2003.

scinteco is an EN ISO 9001:2015-certified company for the design, development, distribution and support of medical software.

how we work

We ensure the best results for our customers, and the optimal conditions for our employees are the reason for this enthusiasm and satisfaction. Among our team, we are treated with respect, integrity and kindness. All of this contributes to a satisfactory collaboration not only with our team but also with our customers. Furthermore, we are proud of the low turnover within our team and value the long-lasting relationships we have with our clients.

our mission

We are devoted to providing leading edge solutions for data management and collaboration supporting the drug development process.
